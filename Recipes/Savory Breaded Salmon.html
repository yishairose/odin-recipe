Salvory Breaded Salmon

Extremely easy to prepare; this dish will become a family favorite. Irresistible!

Delicious crunchy salmon with amazing source. Perfect for an enjoyable Shabbos meal!

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices salmon or 4 fillets
  • garlic powder, for sprinkling
  • paprika, for sprinkling
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons mustard
  • Gefen Cornflake Crumbs (use gluten-free if needed), to coat fish

Steps:

  1. Sprinkle fish with garlic and paprika. Combine oil, sugar, mustard, and vinegar. Mix well. Set aside half the mixture.
  2. Dip fish into remaining half of mixture and then into corn flakes, until coated.
  3. Bake, covered, at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 30 minutes and then an additional 10 minutes uncovered. Top with reserved mixture and serve.
